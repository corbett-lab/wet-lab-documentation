Lysis Buffer (40 mLs)


Final concentration of solution:
* 50mM Tris-HCl (pH = 8)
* 100 mM EDTA
* 100 mM NaCl
* .5% SDS


Reagents: 


* 2 mL 1M Tris-HCl (pH = 8)
* 1.49 g EDTA
* .234 g NaCl
* 4 mL 5% SDS
* ~40 mLs mqH2O


Instructions:
1. Add EDTA and NaCl into 50 mL Falcon tube
2. Add Tris and SDS
3. Fill to 40 mL with mqH2O
4. Vortex thoroughly to dissolve all solutes.
